Bridges

A project about seeing and photographing bridges, and telling a story with those images. My method was to snap as many pictures as possible and work on them with photoshop afterwards to make them tell a story. I learnt about leading space, color pallets and the basics of photoshop.

Copywrite Job was to take a copywrited photo from the net — and artistically tweak it — so as to render the copywrite void.I learnt the basics of Copywrite law, and more practice with Photoshop.
Bridges Double trouble is part of the Bridges project. More practice with Photoshop, specifically copying and transforming images, color rendering, and merging images together.
Who am I? This collage is the front page of the first project I did first week of first semester at CPH Business. The task was to give an impression of myself with no restrictions on method. I opted for a collage followed by descriptive text, that was then presented by my group.
Copywrite This image follows suit with the other Copywrite excercise in which a copywrtied image was to be artisically altered to void copywrite. This particular image gave me practice with the selection tools and stamp tools of photoshop.
Bridges Part of the bridges project — Brotherly love — was my presentaion as a completion of the excercise.I actually completed this photo — which is slightly surreal — before I had completed training with basics of photoshop, and hand inked in all the edges of the supperimposed muriel onto what was seawater in the original.
HTML & CSS Current project and by far the most challenging. I overestimated my abilities to effectively use all the CSS I had studied in the introductory of the excercise, so I chose to restart half way through and build upon a very basic HTML5 framework — Which I was more comfortable with — and use whatever remaining time I had to Style the sheets. Focusing more on functionality that design for the time being
XD Prototyping Preceeding the current project, was a prototyping excercise, which would allow to explore — using Adobe XD — what kind of portfolio we would like to present ourselves and our work through. I was a quick and effective tool, if not misrepresenting a bit what a challenge the same process would be using HTML/CSS.
Layout The layout project was based more on tried and tested design guidelines, such as Grids & Columns, The golden ratio, The rule of thirds, Leading space, Color theory and much more. We were to take a webpage, and through simple restructuring make it more alluring to the human eye.
